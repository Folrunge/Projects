My Projects

Since 2017 I was contracted by a British owned XTM International with a task of providing a technical support. Being a part of XTM exposed me to various tools used in the industry like JIRA, Git, PSQL. It enabled me to further my own knowledge about Linux, web applications and development flow. I also took the initiative and by my own started training newcomers in my team- this helped to reduce their habituation period. Although I briefly touched Java while working in XTM, I decided to push myself in 2019 towards front-end technologies.

Steven Erikson tribute page was my very first page created. Looking back I can find some insufficiencies in its code and structure, but I decided to keep it for some reason.

Random quote generator was constructed as a part of FCC challenge. As its name suggests, it takes a random quote found on the Internet and pastes it on a page with a tweet option available.

Weather app was my first take on implementing AJAX for communication with an external API. In function it's similar to a standard weather app on your regular smartphone.

Wikipedia viewer was my latest project during the FCC. By using AJAX, I created a page that worked as a search engine for Wikipedia articles.
